# Privacy Policy Last updated: 5/1/25 ## Introduction FPL Guru ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. ## Information We Collect ### FPL Team Data - FPL Team ID (when provided) - Team selection and transfer history - Team performance statistics ### Device Information - Device type and operating system - App usage statistics - Notification preferences ### How We Use Your Information We use the collected information to: - Display your FPL team data and statistics - Generate team recommendations - Send deadline notifications (if enabled) - Improve app functionality and user experience ### Data Storage - All data is stored locally on your device - No personal information is transmitted to external servers - FPL team data is fetched directly from the official FPL API ### Advertising We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our app. AdMob may collect and process data including: - Device information - Advertising ID - IP address - Location data (if permitted) - App usage data for ad targeting For more information about how AdMob processes data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings: - Android: Google Settings > Ads > Opt out of personalized advertising - iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track" ### Third-Party Services We use the following third-party services: - Official Fantasy Premier League API - Expo Services for app functionality ### Your Rights You can: - Enable/disable notifications - Remove your FPL team ID from the app - Clear all locally stored data ### Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. ### Contact Us If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at monospheredev@gmail.com.
